PROBLEM

A country divided

Our nation is faced with deep divisions within the populace, low trust and understanding of its institutions, and (until the election of 2020) one of the lowest voter turnout among the world's democracies. .

SOLUTION

A guide to increasing civic awareness and historical understanding

An initiative conceived by a partnership between iCivics, the School of Civic & Economic Thought and Leadership at Arizona State University, the Edmond J. Safra Center for Ethics at Harvard University, the Center for Information and Research on Civic Learning and Engagement (CIRCLE) at Tufts University, and the Jonathan M. Tisch College of Civic Life at Tufts University
