INSPIRATIONS

Each step along my path has been a step towards being a more compassionate, empathetic, and effective designer.

From a young age, I've been in love with comics. I spent hours creating my own, meticulously laying out panels to tell a story. I learned that good storytelling necessitates good design.

Growing up as a middle child in an immigrant family has shaped me in more ways than one. A natural mediator and I've learned the power of inclusivity, belonging, and compassion.

From teaching hundreds of students from various backgrounds, I've learned the value of empathy and understanding, and how to cater lesson plans according to people's backgrounds. Context is king.
