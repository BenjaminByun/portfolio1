ABOUT ME

My diverse work history has been my greatest mentor.

I’ve produced reality television, scripted shows, and blockbuster movies in Hollywood, solving one production problem after another. I’ve also produced nationally-syndicated commercials at Hasbro, a global toy glomerate, while acting as a liason between the marketing teams, the creative department, and the production crews. Lastly, I’ve taught English as a second language across two continents and three metropolitan cities, from overworked elementary school students in Korea to 60-year-old heart surgeons from Belarus.

Each job required the ability to recognize and define problems, the creativity to come up with out-of-the-box solutions, and collaboration with headstrong egos (read: Hollywood) from all walks of life. They also required a deep empathy and understanding of both those you work with and the target audience. I believe all these skills tie directly into being an effective UX UI designer.
