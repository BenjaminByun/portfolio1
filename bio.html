Soft skills

Inclusive

As the son of immigrant parents, I've often felt like a man without a country. Not Korean enough to be Korean, not White enough to be American, I longed for acceptance and community. From this existential limbo, I've learned the importance of inclusivity, belonging, and compassion. These values are essential when advocating for users in user-centered design.

Empathetic

As a middle child, I was inadvertently assigned the role of famliy mediator. To be an efficacious mediator, one needs to be an active and engaged listener; an understanding of character and context is a must; finally, above all else, one needs to practice endless empathy. Coincidentally, these are the traits of an effective UX designer as well.
